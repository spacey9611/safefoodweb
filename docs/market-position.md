*Assessed after the full build: 650 unique questions (40% scenario), state-aware quiz, 52 schema-rich pages, GA4 wired, GSC verification in progress.*

---

## Verdict in one line
**On product depth you are already the best dedicated food-safety practice test in Australia. You are NOT yet the market dominator (zero authority/age/backlinks), and you are not yet a money-maker (AdSense + affiliates not live). The path to both is now off-page + monetisation wiring, not more content.**

It is a strong, compounding asset — realistic ceiling €1.5–5k/mo for this single site over 6–18 months. The "true money-maker" is the **repeatable template** (White Card proven → Food Safety now → RSA/others next), not one site.

## Is it a true money-maker? (honest)
- **Model is sound:** AU is Tier-1 for AdSense; education RPM ~$5–15, and your 8–15 min quiz sessions push the upper half. Affiliate courses pay $15–50/referral (3–5× White Card).
- **But nothing earns yet:** ad slots are placeholders (not live AdSense units), affiliate programs aren't joined, and the site is brand-new with no rankings. Revenue is currently $0 and will stay near $0 until traffic + monetisation are switched on.
- **Ceiling is niche-capped:** ~48k addressable searches/mo at full ranking → base ~€1.5–2k/mo by month 12, upside ~€5k/mo. Excellent solo asset; not a fortune from one site.
