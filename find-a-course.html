Find a Food Safety Course

Practice tests prepare you for the knowledge component, but official SITXFSA005 (food handler) or SITXFSA006 (Food Safety Supervisor) certificates must be issued by a registered training organisation (RTO).

Popular accredited providers

  • Australian Institute of Food Safety (AIFS) — typical price $199–$230
  • Clear To Work — typical price $86–$125
  • Allens Training — typical price $85–$110
  • Club Training Australia — typical price $95–$120

What to look for

  • Nationally recognised unit codes (SITXFSA005 or SITXFSA006)
  • RTO listed on training.gov.au
  • Certificate valid in your state
  • FSS certificate renewed every 5 years

Food safety course FAQ

How much does a food safety certificate cost?+
Food handler (SITXFSA005) courses are often $25–$60 online; Food Safety Supervisor (SITXFSA005 + SITXFSA006) courses typically run $85–$230. See our cost by state guide.
Is an online food safety certificate valid?+
Yes, when it is issued by an accredited RTO for the nationally recognised units. Verify the provider on training.gov.au. NSW also runs its own approved-RTO scheme.
Which unit do I need — SITXFSA005 or SITXFSA006?+
Food handlers need SITXFSA005. Food Safety Supervisors complete both SITXFSA005 and SITXFSA006. See FSS vs food handler.
How long does the course take?+
Many online food handler courses take only a few hours, since they are self-paced. Preparing with our free practice test first helps you pass first time.
